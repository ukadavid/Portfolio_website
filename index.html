About Me

Hi, I'm Tobechukwu Uka, a self-taught front-end developer from Nigeria.

I build websites with a focus on responsiveness, accessibility

I first started coding sometime in 2019 after I and my mentor “Godsman” had a little conversation and in 2 months I built my first website. A single-paged food delivery website (legacy website here for posterity) and from then, I was hooked.

I knew I enjoyed giving people beautiful experiences when they navigated my website, so it was a no brainer that frontend development was for me. That's how I started my career in front end development.

I am constantly trying to learn new technologies and concepts to take my career to another level. I also started sharing my knowledge by writing articles, mentoring and teaching people. In the future, I hope to speak at tech conferences around the world.

I enjoy writing, reading and playing chess.

Currently, I am focused on my career and I am also intentional about my growth. I know that if I continue to put in the time and work, in 10-15 years, I should be a staff engineer at a company I love.

Thanks for reading.
