About Me

Hi, I'm Tobechukwu Uka, a self-taught front-end developer from Nigeria.

My coding journey began in late 2020, when I decided to transition from business to tech. Since then, I have been on an exciting journey of learning, which has solidified my decision to pursue a career in software engineering. What started out as a curious interest quickly evolved into passion for design, innovation, and problem-solving.

I am currently enrolled into the Junior frontend development nanodegree which I will be done with by May, 2022. I am open to opportunities because I believe that working in an industry will give me the exposure I need while utilizing the knowledge learnt during the program. My current stack includes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. In addition, I have worked with Bootstrap as well as designing web solutions with Figma. I am currently in the process of learning React.
